9 Mckelvy 2 social studies Kaitiakitanga

Direct translation of Kaitiakitanga - A person, group, or being that acts as a guardian or protector to something else e.g. Land, Tapu, Taunga, or Ancestors, someone who carries out an action. But there is deeper meaning than what was explained here. Guardianship, protection, preservation. Guardianship over tapu like pounamu, and sacred things. Kaitiakitanga is spiritual/physical guardianship over a person or thing. It can be being a mother, older sibling, or teacher. This means you are a kaitiaki for others.
